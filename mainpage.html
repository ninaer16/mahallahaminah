History of Mahallah Aminah

Mahallah Aminah takes the name of Aminah bint Wahb, the mother of the Islamic Prophet Muhammad s.a.w. Aminah originated in Yathrib (Madinah) among the Bani Zuhra tribe. When the Prophet Muhammad s.a.w. was just six years old, Aminah grew ill and perished at the city of al-‘Abwa while travelling to see the grave of his father, Abdullah ibn Abd al-Muttalib.
